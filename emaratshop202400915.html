About the Project

This deep cleaning project for the newly constructed Emarat Petrol Pump Shop in Business Bay was a critical assignment, completed within a tight one-day deadline to meet the shop's grand opening. Our team of 10 highly skilled staff worked diligently to ensure the space was spotless and ready for business.

The project involved:

  • Initial consultation with the client to understand specific cleaning requirements and expectations.
  • Detailed planning and coordination to ensure all tasks were completed in a single day.
  • A comprehensive cleaning strategy, addressing every corner of the shop, including the interior and exterior areas.
  • Deployment of advanced cleaning equipment to guarantee efficiency and high standards.

Our team’s careful planning and flawless execution ensured that the project was completed on time, leaving the shop in perfect condition for its grand opening in Business Bay.
